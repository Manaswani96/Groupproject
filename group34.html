Sustainable Aquaculture for Small-Scale Farmers

Water Conservation

Using recirculating aquaculture systems (RAS), fish farmers can reduce water wastage by reusing water multiple times, ensuring sustainability.

Integrated Multi-Trophic Aquaculture

IMTA involves farming different species together, using the by-products of one to support others, improving sustainability and ecosystem health.

Eco-Friendly Feed

Plant-based and alternative feed options reduce the need for fishmeal, which helps protect marine ecosystems.

Sustainable Fish Species Selection

Choosing species that are well-suited to the local environment and require fewer resources ensures sustainability in aquaculture systems.

Local and Small-Scale Aquaculture

Local, small-scale aquaculture reduces transportation emissions and supports local economies, ensuring a sustainable food production system.
